About

Hello! My name is Huynh Nhu Ngoc (Jaden), and I am a passionate web developer with a strong focus on frontend technologies. I enjoy turning creative ideas into visually engaging and user-friendly web applications. My expertise lies in Vue, React, and .NET 8 (Minimal API), and I am constantly improving my skills to write efficient, scalable, and maintainable code. I have worked on various projects, including a CV Creator web application, a Recycling Sokoban game, and a support request system for UEH. Through these projects, I have gained experience in building interactive UI components, optimizing performance, and integrating APIs to create seamless user experiences. Beyond technical skills, I am highly adaptable, eager to learn, and always ready to take on new challenges. My goal is to become a professional developer who contributes long-term to innovative projects and makes a meaningful impact in the field of web development. Currently, I am preparing for my upcoming internship, focusing on improving my coding logic and problem-solving abilities. In the future, I aspire to pursue a Master’s degree in Computer Science abroad with a full scholarship to further deepen my knowledge and expertise. I am always open to collaboration and new opportunities. If you’re looking for someone who is dedicated, detail-oriented, and passionate about web development, feel free to reach out!
